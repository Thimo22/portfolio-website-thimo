Project Waste Reduction

I had to do research to waste reduction.
This is a "made up problem" to let us make a case study where show the steps that are needed to research / design / code an app.
As a problem definition they gave: The global waste crisis continues to grow, with individuals and businesses often lacking clear guidance on waste reduction and recycling.
How can we create a mobile app that encourages waste reduction, promotes recycling, and provides comprehensive waste management information?

Explanation

In the case study, I show all the steps I took from begin to end in how to make an app.
After researching (with different research methodes) the stakeholders needs we can come up with a design, after some iterations we can go up in the design stages (sketch, low-fidelity, mid-fidelity, hi-fidelity).
I mainly use Figma to make my mid-fidelity and hi-fidelity prototypes (so I can test my prototypes to the stakeholders)
After design we go and make it in code, in this case we had to make it PWA (Progressive Web App).
