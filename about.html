About Me

I’m Thimo van der Vlies, a 23-year-old ICT & Media Design student at the Fontys University of Applied Sciences in Eindhoven. I currently live in Helmond. I love to make designs, and I am beginning to enjoy coding more and more.
I choose ICT & Media design because I really like doing creative things, it makes me feel happy especially if I can help another person.
When I busy with my projects I dive in the deep and try to make the best thing I can at that moment...

I currently work part-time at Jumbo in the AGF department, where I’ve developed skills in customer service, teamwork, and time management.
I love doing my job that's why I work there for already 6 years and counting.
I’m eager to apply my knowledge in real-world projects and contribute to innovative solutions for ICT.

Now that I feel good enough, I am motivated to find a internship where I can utilize my skills.

As for now I want to become a UI/UX Designer or maybe a Front-End Developer in the future.
I have more projects that I have made, but I have chosen the ones that had a special meaning to me, you can find those on the projects page.
